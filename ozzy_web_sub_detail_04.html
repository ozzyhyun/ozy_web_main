Mobile UI/UX Renewal, Field Research, Released Service
2022.04 - 2022.06
I participated in a one-day field study to identify the issues faced by users who had never used the app before. From this research, I found several problems. For instance, the same UX writings were repeated multiple times on a single screen due to limited function implementation.

Additionally, there was a visualization issue where the difference between the actual vehicle and the 3D mock-up in the app. Due to that, first-time users are confused of which vehicle they should take on or cannot distinguish the color or shape of the vehicle come from.

To address these issues, I suggested simplifying the UX strings and updating the multiple 3D mock-up images. Furthermore, to increase revenue, I suggested using commercial methods, such as displaying YouTube videos or music videos, in the vacant areas of the screen.
